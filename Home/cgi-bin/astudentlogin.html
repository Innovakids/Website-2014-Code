Educational Philosophy:

The concept behind Innovakids.com is to create an inexpensive site where teachers can share lessons, quizzes and student information. This site includes many features all inclusive in one site allowing teachers and staff to coordinate curriculum, lessons, schedules, assignments, discipline, grades homework, parent communication, online books and more.
